== Introduction and Goals

This blogfolio project aims to create a website that serves two primary purposes: a personal blog for sharing programming-related knowledge and a professional portfolio showcasing personal projects and skills.

=== Requirements Overview

* Functional Requirements:
** Blogging Platform:
*** Allow creation and publication of articles on various programming topics.
*** Support rich text formatting (e.g., code blocks, images, lists).
*** Enable categorization and tagging of blog posts for easy navigation.
** Portfolio Website:
*** Feature an "About Me" section detailing skills, experience, and contact information.
*** Provide a clear and intuitive navigation structure between the blog and portfolio sections.
** User Interface:
*** A responsive and aesthetically pleasing design that works well across different devices (desktop, tablet, mobile).
*** Clear and consistent branding.
